Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register.

01 Where is DevConf 2026 taking place?

The conference will be held at the Moscone Center, San Francisco. Full venue details, maps, and nearby hotel recommendations are available on the Venue page.

02 What's included in my ticket?

Every ticket includes access to keynotes and technical talks, workshop sessions, networking lunches, and coffee breaks. Higher tiers add perks like speaker meet and greets and lifetime recordings — see the pricing section above for a full breakdown.

03 Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket?

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your registration to a colleague at no extra cost by contacting our support team.

04 Will sessions be recorded?

Yes. All main-stage talks are recorded and made available to Pro and Team ticket holders. Standard ticket holders get 30 days of digital access to recordings.

05 Do you offer group or student discounts?

Yes — teams of 5 or more can save with our Team plan, and verified students receive 50% off Standard tickets. Reach out through the Contact Us button for a discount code.
